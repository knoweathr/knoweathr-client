About knoWeathr

One of the great problems that plagues us in the 21st century is the difficulty of planning a successful trip. The biggest variable is always the weather. Taking this into consideration, our team decided to design an app which can help. We use weather data which looks at past trends in the weather to help you decide where to travel based on what kind of weather you prefer.

Will J. Reid began his coding education in a one-room schoolhouse in the scenic Pacific Northwest. The schoolhouse was located in the backyard of his actual house. His first courses were all online; he studied late into the night, his only illumination coming from the very computer he was using for Code Academy courses.

In July of 2017, Will joined the August cohort for the Code Fellows course introducing the Foundations of Software Development. That course, followed by the Intermediate Software Development course, allow Will to be a more effective product manager of custom software solutions, including web-based and iOS native apps, for the Software Development department at American Homes 4 Rent.

After all of hard-earned lessons about object-oriented programming, Heroku deployments and database queries, Will is ready for a vacation. Fortunately, with the knoWeathr app in hand, he can figure out just where to go for a relaxing time in an 80-degree climate.
